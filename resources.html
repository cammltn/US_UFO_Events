Government Documents: The Black Vault

The data below was compiled and digitized by The Black Vault. The organization has aimed to improve accessability for the thousands of government documents released in the past year. Clicking the link in the Download column will open a scanneed PDF document with government redactions. Each document has a brief description also provided by The Black Vault detailing the contents of each document.

CIA FOIA Documents
Download File Size Description
C05515622 477K Two page memo from the CIA’s Domestic Collection Division regarding “UFO Research” – April 26, 1976
C05515624 396K One page document regarding “UFO Research/ORD Request for Additional Information.” The record talks about the work of an unknown individual, along with a system that may assist in the UFO research. The system is redacted. – June 25, 1976
C05515625 385K A one page, “Confidential” message to the HQ of the CIA’s Domestic Collection Division. It is regarding a UFO case, and UFO research, citing material that “should remain classified” but the person wanted the advice of “CIA UFO Experts.”
C05515646 551K A September 1976 record, heavily redacted, which talked about a UFO sighted in Morocco.
C05515647 390K A one page message regarding an unknown case number, seeking progress made. The message refers to the people at Wright Field (present day Wright Patterson Air Force Base) “holding their breaths awaiting advise.” – May 1957
C05515648 425K A one page message, wherein it references Dr. Leon Davidson, and his request seeking an updated on a “tape” being analyzed by Wright Field, likely Project Blue Book. Date unknown.
C05515649 382K A one page message updating on the Leon Davidson situation, and the fact Davidson was sent a letter response. – 1957.
C05515650 420K A 1957 document referencing Dr. Leon Davidson, and his request for an identification of a “Space Message.” The message appears they are frustrated that Davidson will not take the answer that the message was morse code.
C05515651 417K A 1957 document, also referring to Dr. Leon Davidson’s “space message.” Davidson continued to show frustration about the lack of answers he was receiving.
C05515652 416K A 1958 message concerning Leon Davidson, and the articles he was publishing about “flying saucers” and the CIA.
C05515653 394K A 1967 message regarding the film “UFO Fact or Fancy” – likely because the CIA was contacted for the production.
C05515654 413K A 1957 message regarding Dr. Leon Davidson, and his push for answers.
C05515655 397K 1957 message concerning Dr. Leon Davidson, and the fact he would get a reply to his inquiries.
C05515656 404K December 1957 letter outline Dr. Leon Davidson’s persistence, and the recommendation he is now ignored.
C05515657 406K A 1958 document that displays the frustration about Dr. Leon Davidson, and the fact that the agency wants to no longer be involved with the matter.
C05515658 414K A 13 August 1957 intelligence report about Unidentified Flying Objects over Iran.
C05515659 452K A 22 October 1959 intelligence report about “Possible Unidentified Flying Object” in the Ukraine.
C05515660 402K A 9 March 1960 intelligence information report about “Rumored Unidentified Flying Objects in the Sary Ozek Region.”
C05515661 427K A 25 May 1962 document, which chronicles Unidentified Flying Objects in Buenos Aires, Argentina. This is utilizing information, as published by the local newspapers at the time of the event.
C05515662 423K A July 1965 document, outlining “Antarctic Flying Saucers” over Deception Island. This intelligence was gathered from the media.
C05515663 468K A 23 September 1977 document, which outlines “Unusual Natural Phenomenon observed in Karelia.” For 10-12 minutes, an object described as a star, went over the city and shined down bright light and “very fine rays” which created an image of pouring rain.
